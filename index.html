About Me

I am a highly driven and multi-talented individual, excelling in both academics and athletics. As a student at VIT Chennai, my passion for robotics has led me to participate in prestigious competitions like the International Rover Challenge (IRC) 2024 and Robocon 2025, where I have showcased my technical expertise and problem-solving skills.

My dedication to innovation extends to AI, as I am currently developing a system to correct body posture during gym exercises. Beyond academics, I am a formidable athlete, having been the highest wicket-taker in Gautam Buddh Nagar in 2018 and earning the Man of the Match title in the Sairam Sports Meet 2023.

I have also displayed exceptional versatility in sports, winning a district-level gold medal in karate, excelling in badminton at the district level, and securing first place in calisthenics. With a blend of intellect, discipline, and athleticism, I continue to push boundaries and inspire others in both the technological and sporting arenas.
